About Me

Game Development is my passion. Over the last two years I worked a lot with Unity and C# on different projects solo and within a team. I also have experience with Unreal, C++ and other programming languages like C and Java. My focus lies on programming, but I have also gained valuable experience in game design. Most of the following projects were created in my spare time for learning purposes.

Projects

Into The Depth

This game is a mix of Diablo-like Hack and Slay with the Rogue-like genre. You enter a procedurally generated dungeon, gather loot and grind levels to get stronger. This solo project was the biggest I've worked on so far with different systems like a character stat system and an item system with modifiers and different rarity levels.

Cybergolf

This game was a fifth semester university project. Like the name implies, it is a simple golf game in neon-cyberpunk look, but with a little twist: some properties such as the elasticity of the ball and a special skill are randomly selected at the beginning of a game.

Full Control

This little puzzle game was my first bigger solo project. Like in every good puzzler you have different levels with increasing difficulty. The core concept is, that you first have to carefully plan how you want to solve the level. Afterward you have to stick to this plan and execute it precisely, because every move you make is important and one little mistake can destroy your plan.
