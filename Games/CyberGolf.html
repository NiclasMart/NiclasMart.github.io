Cybergolf

Description

The game was a team project of three as part of the fifth semester at university. The task was to create a small sport-related game. So we chose mini golf as our topic, similar to 'Golf with your friends' or ' Golf it'. The main goal of the game doesn't differ from other golf games. However, the twist is that you are assigned a random skill on each playthrough and the elasticity of the ball and the gravity are randomized. There are three maps in total and each of them offers different paths to the hole, which are more or less difficult with different abilities and properties. Thus, each playthrough feels different.
