Tactil Interaction Project - Immersion

Description

This research project was developed during the 4th semester in the module "Development Tools for Rich Internet Applications" with the main topic "Tactile Interaction". Our task was to find and research an application area for vibrotactile feedback. Our team, consisting of three people, decided for the topic "Increasing immersion in video games and other digital media through emotion mediation using vibrotactile feedback". As final milestone of our project, we developed a small VR environment in Unity with a horror setting. For the vibrotactile Feedback we used the bHaptics Tactsuit which offers an API for Unity. In our test, we simulated the character's heartbeat and used different vibration patterns to represent fear, tension and other emotional feedback.
