Battle Tanks Arena

Description

This little game was a gamejam-like project to learn how to work with Unreal Engine and develop an understanding of the workflow, which is a little different from the Unity workflow. Each round, the player must take out all enemy towers. Their spawn locations are randomly generated and the map's structure changes a bit from round to round. The player must get through all five rounds without dying to win the game. Each round gets a little more difficult.
