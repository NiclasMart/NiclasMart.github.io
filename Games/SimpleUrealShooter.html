Simple Unreal Shooter

Description

This was the first shooter-like game I programmed. It has simple game mechanics such as two different weapons to switch between, simple enemy AI achieved through the Unreal Behavior Tree system and a critical hit mechanism. The game was developed in one week and is more of a prototype for a potentially larger and more complex game.
